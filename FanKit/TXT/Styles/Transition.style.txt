

###Entrance:

```xaml



<ContentControl x:Name="EntranceContentControl" >
    <ContentControl.ContentTransitions>
        <TransitionCollection>
            <EntranceThemeTransition FromVerticalOffset="200"/>
        </TransitionCollection>
    </ContentControl.ContentTransitions>
    <!--Content-->
</ContentControl>

```

```Csharp
this.EntranceContentControl.Content = new TextBlock();
    

```
